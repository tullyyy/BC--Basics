Boulder, Colorado

This page is to show a little of the beauty of Boulder, Colorado. Boulder is a "small" community (300,000 people) at the foothills of The Rocky Mountains. It is known for its snowy mountains and outdoorsy people. The city is over a mile high in elevation (1,655 m) and is host to a large university. Come visit! There is a large art scene and many breweries. Want to know more?
